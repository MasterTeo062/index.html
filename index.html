Who Can Join?

Any player aged 10–18 based in or around Littlehampton, West Sussex. All ability levels welcome — whether you've never played or train regularly.

Where Do We Train?

We are currently scouting local grounds in the Littlehampton area. Training location will be confirmed once we have our first squads together.

Is It Free?

Signing up costs nothing. Once the club is more established we may introduce small session fees to cover pitch hire, but we'll always keep it affordable.

Non-Official Club

United Apex FC is a brand new grassroots club, not currently affiliated with the FA. We play friendly and casual matches while we build the club up.
