How it works

✅
All Abilities Welcome
Whether you've played since you were tiny or you've never been on a pitch before, we welcome you. United Apex FC is about developing players, not just picking the best ones.
👟
What to Bring
Football boots or trainers, a water bottle, and a good attitude. That's genuinely all you need to come to your first session. No kit required to start.
👨‍👩‍👦
Parents Welcome
Parents and guardians are always welcome to watch sessions at Brookfield. We believe in keeping families involved and informed about everything happening at the club.
📋
Trial Sessions
Every new player starts with a few trial sessions so both they and we can see how things fit. After that we'll confirm their place in the squad and get them properly registered.

Ready to sign up?

Fill in the sign-up form and we'll be in touch about your first session. Takes less than two minutes.



Training Ground

Brookfield

Brookfield on Worthing Road is our training base while we get the club off the ground. It's a large open green space in Littlehampton — free to use, no booking required, and easy to get to from across town.

📍 Worthing Road, Littlehampton, BN17
📍 Get Directions →
🟢
No Booking Needed
Brookfield is a public open space — no need to call ahead or reserve anything. Just show up at the right time and we'll be there.
🚶
Easy to Reach
Located on Worthing Road, Brookfield is walkable from most parts of Littlehampton and close to local bus routes. Parking is also available nearby.
⚽
Grass Pitches
Brookfield has large open grass areas that are perfect for small-sided games, drills, and full training sessions. Plenty of room for all age groups.
🏗️
Temporary Home
As we grow and get FA affiliated, we plan to secure a permanent booked pitch. Brookfield is where we start — our first proper home will come in time.

First Session Details

Tuesday 9th June 2026 · 17:00

Meet at Brookfield, Worthing Road. Look for the group in red. Bring boots or trainers, water, and yourself. No kit required. All ages can come along.
