Training Ground

Brookfield Park

📍 Worthing Road, Littlehampton, BN17

Why Brookfield?

Our Local Base

Brookfield Park is a large open green space in Littlehampton with football-friendly grass areas. As a new grassroots club, it gives us a free, accessible place to start training, build team spirit, and get the ball rolling before we secure a permanent home.

Open Access

No booking required for casual training sessions. Just show up and play.

Central Location

Easily accessible from across Littlehampton, close to public transport.

Temporary Home

We plan to move to a booked pitch once squads are established and funded.

Be Part of History

Founding Members

United Apex FC is brand new. We have no history yet — which means you can be part of making it. Founding members are the first people to sign up, the ones who believed before anyone else did.

Your name will be recorded as a founder of this club. When we win our first trophy, when we field our first team, when we look back at how this all started — founding members will be remembered.

Only 50 founding member spots available. Once they're gone, they're gone.

Who Can Join?

Any player aged 10–18 in or around Littlehampton, West Sussex. All ability levels welcome — beginners and experienced players alike.

Where Do We Train?

Initially at Brookfield Park, Worthing Road, Littlehampton — a large open public park. Free to use, no booking needed.

Is It Free?

Signing up is completely free. We may introduce small pitch fees in future, but we'll always keep it accessible for everyone.

Non-Official Club

United Apex FC is not FA affiliated. We play friendly and casual matches while building toward official status. Real football, real community.
